Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the School Management Information System (SMIS) collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information Collected

The system may collect names, emails, student records, academic results, and financial information necessary for school administration.

2. Use of Information

Information collected is used strictly for educational management and administrative purposes.

3. Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user information against unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Confidentiality

User data will not be shared with unauthorized third parties except where required by law or school policy.

5. User Rights

Users may request correction of inaccurate information through the school administration.

6. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised periodically.
